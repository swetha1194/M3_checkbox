Research grants

Grants, in the form of sponsored prizes, are available for researchers hosting their first experiment.

Eligiblity for a grant includes meeting at least one criterion:

  • New Phd student.
  • Creative experiment ideas.
  • Non-traditional researcher (developer wanting to try research)

Contact Chris Parnin if you would like to ask about or apply for an research grant.
